Health First

All our dogs are health tested to the highest standards. We screen for BAER, CAER, patella, cardiac and thyroid conditions before any breeding takes place.

Show Quality

We breed to the Havanese standard and show our dogs at Championship level. Our stud dog Spike has won Best Veteran at Crufts 2025 and 2026.

Home Raised

All our puppies are born and raised in our home, fully socialised from day one. They leave us well prepared for life as a beloved family companion.
