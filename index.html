A small, serious kennel

Alphapups Havanese is a show kennel based in the UK, producing around five to six litters per year. Every dog is raised in our home as part of the family.

Exceptional lines

We work with some of the finest lines in the breed, including the leased American Champion That's Boy Wonder at Lake Michigan, bringing outstanding type and pedigree to our programme.

Health first

All our breeding dogs are health tested to the highest standards. We believe that only by testing thoroughly can we be confident in the long-term health of our puppies and the breed.
