Bobby

AM CH That's Boy Wonder at Lake Michigan

Born: 2019  |  Leased from David & Darlene Skiris

Bobby is our leased American Champion stud dog, bringing exceptional type, coat, and showmanship to the Alphapups breeding programme. An American Champion with a proven show record in the USA, Bobby is a striking black and white parti Havanese with outstanding conformation and a wonderful temperament.

His influence on our breeding programme has been immediate and significant — his litter with Poppy in January 2026 produced eight beautiful puppies, demonstrating both his fertility and his ability to pass on his exceptional qualities. We are delighted to have him at Alphapups and look forward to seeing his offspring develop.

Bobby is health tested to the highest standards and his pedigree combines some of the finest American Havanese bloodlines.
