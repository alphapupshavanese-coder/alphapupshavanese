Bambi

Alphapups Bambino

Breeding Female  |  Katie x Spike  |  1 Litter (May 2025)

Bambi is one of our home-bred girls — a daughter of Katie and Spike, combining two of our very best lines. She is a beautiful white Havanese with an exceptional coat, wonderful temperament, and all the qualities we aim for in our breeding programme.

Bambi has already qualified for Crufts and continues to develop into a promising show dog. Her first litter in May 2025 produced lovely puppies, demonstrating she is as capable in the whelping box as she is in the ring.

We have very high hopes for Bambi's future both in the show ring and as a breeding female, and she is a dog we are enormously proud to have bred ourselves.

Judge's Critiques

"Once this baby gains in confidence she will take some beating I am sure. Such a sweet nature and a beautifully balanced head with a typical expression and large, dark, almond eyes. She has fabulous pigmentation for such a pale cream coat making her even more attractive. Her body is compact and just the right length for her height. Her coat was well presented, it is developing well for her age and is of silky texture. Sound away and back. Very promising indeed."

Nick Gourley — LKA 2023, Puppy Bitch (2nd)

"Lovely feminine head, a superbly constructed girl with an excellent topline, lovely outgoing girl that enjoys the ring, coat of excellent texture and presented in lovely condition. I see she is the daughter of my Reserve Dog CC winner."

Stephen Randle Goodwin (Lanola) — Manchester Championship Show 2026, Postgraduate Bitch (1st)
