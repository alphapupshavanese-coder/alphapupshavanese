Katie

Alphapups Dutches Daisy Pudding

Breeding Female  |  3 Litters  |  2 CCs

Katie is one of our most successful breeding females and show dogs. With two Challenge Certificates to her name and three litters producing exceptional puppies, she is at the heart of the Alphapups programme.

Her show career has taken her to the highest level of UK competition, winning CCs and placing consistently at Championship Shows. Her type, coat and temperament are everything we strive for in a Havanese, and she passes these qualities reliably to her offspring.

Katie is health tested to the highest standards and her puppies are much sought after by both show homes and loving pet families.
