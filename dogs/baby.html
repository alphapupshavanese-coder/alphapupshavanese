Baby

Katie's Sister  |  Breeding Female

Baby is Katie's sister and a key part of the Alphapups breeding programme. A striking chocolate and tan Havanese with a wonderful temperament, Baby brings exceptional type and a rich, dark coat to our litters.

Baby has had a successful show career in her own right and her puppies are consistently of excellent quality. Her show wins include placements at Championship level, and she has qualified for Crufts on multiple occasions.

Baby is fully health tested and her combination of exceptional coat, type and character makes her an invaluable part of the Alphapups kennel.
